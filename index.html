Built on Craft

ANCO Studio sits at 1A/120 Customs Street West in the Viaduct Harbour — the heart of Auckland's waterfront. We're a luxury yet accessible hair salon for people who value exceptional colour, cut, and style.

Director Adrian Deng is Auckland's Best Colourist for Blondes, with over ten years of experience. Our team are official ambassadors for Olaplex and Lakmē, and experts in everything from seamless blondes to rich mahoganies.

Enjoy our unique full-body massage basins while you transform. No apps. No deposits. Just walk in and leave renewed.
